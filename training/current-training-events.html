Upcoming training events

This page contains upcoming events from the NeIC training calendar, which includes both NeIC training and stakeholder training which NeIC either participates in or simply wants to advertize. Please refer to the official website for each training for accurate and up-to-date information on for example admittance, costs, etc. You may also want to look for past training events. These lists are generated with javascript in your browser, so if you cannot see any events please check your settings.
